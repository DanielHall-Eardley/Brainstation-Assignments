About WD Rascals

Pleasure your eyes with the whimsical story of our creation!

WD Rascals joins us from Paris. The band has a light sound and a golden style, bringing eclectic guitars, jilted rhythm, extreme vocals, and turquoise songwriting to the table. The band has been transversing ears in venues across the world since 1997. WD rascals plays tunes from a variety of genres, and their influences include bands like Wham! and The Killers.
